itFood Admin Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the itFood Admin platform. This policy outlines how we handle the personal and business data of our restaurant partners and administrators.

2. Data We Collect

We collect business information, owner details, contact information, menu data, and financial information necessary to facilitate payouts and platform operations.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to manage your restaurant profile, process customer orders, facilitate financial transactions, and provide analytics on your business performance.

4. Data Sharing and Transparency

We do not share your business data with unauthorized third parties. Information is only shared as required to fulfill service obligations, process payments, or comply with legal requirements.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to prevent your business and customer data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. Access to administrative data is strictly controlled.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your administrative access, please contact us at partners@itfood.in.
